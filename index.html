How It Works

Easiest. Date. Ever.

1

Book Online

Availability is limited, so book early and don't worry – you can cancel or reschedule for free up to 24 hours before or if the weather becomes decidedly un-picnicky. Choose Lunch (Art Hill) or Dinner (Pagoda Lake) below to see available dates.

2

Meet & Grab

Meet us at your location's designated spot — top of Art Hill near the Art Museum for lunch, Pagoda Lake near The Muny for dinner. We'll hand you a fully-stocked picnic basket and blanket with everything you need — just a few steps away from an idyllic spot for your perfect picnic.

3

Enjoy & Relax

Find your ideal spot—sun, shade, whatever you prefer—and spend 2 hours enjoying a three-course meal in one of St. Louis's most beautiful settings. No noisy dining room, no hovering waitstaff. Just you two.
