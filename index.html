St. Louis' Favorite Date

Private Picnic Dates in Forest Park, St. Louis

Forest Park is one of the largest urban parks in America — and one of the most romantic. We've taken the most beautiful setting in St. Louis and made it effortless.

No reservations to fight over. No parking stress. No dishes. Just you, your person, a fully stocked basket, and two hours in one of the most beautiful places in the city.

Every picnic comes with a 3-course meal from one of four rotating local restaurants — all from the Central West End and Grove neighborhoods, keeping your dollars right here in St. Louis.

How It Works

Easiest. Date. Ever.

1

Book Online

Availability is limited, so book early. Don't worry — you can cancel or reschedule for free up to 24 hours before, or if the weather becomes decidedly un-picnicky. Choose Lunch (Art Hill) or Dinner (Pagoda Lake) below to see available dates.

2

Meet & Grab

Meet us at the designated pickup/dropoff spot. We'll hand you a fully-stocked picnic basket and blanket with everything you need — just a few steps away from an idyllic spot for your perfect picnic.

3

Enjoy & Relax

Find your ideal spot — sun, shade, whatever you prefer — and spend 2 hours enjoying a three-course meal in one of St. Louis's most beautiful settings. No noisy dining room, no hovering waitstaff. Just you two.
