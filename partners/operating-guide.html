Partner Operating Guide

Your Entrée Boxes are the stars of the show.
Here's how to prepare them for the spotlight every time.

This guide covers everything that happens once your month is confirmed — from advance notice and order quantities to how guests discover your place and become new regulars.

Online Menu & Blog Feature

Every guest scans a QR code for their digital menu. We include links to your website, Google review page, or any other content you rely on to keep new fans engaged. We also write a feature post on tourcwe.com about your establishment, team, and origin story — or all of the above. We'll work with you to make it great.

Advance Order Notice

Because we accept cancellations up to 12 hours before and orders up to 4 hours before picnic time, we give you 3 hours advance notice of exact order quantity:

8am

for 11am lunch pickups

2pm

for 5pm dinner pickups

Orders are capped at 12 boxes per service. We also share projected sellout dates at the beginning of each week so you can plan accordingly.

Box Drop-off

We come to your service entrance to drop off a thermal bag with the proper number of sanitized & lined stainless steel boxes — a minimum of one hour before (10am / 4pm) your scheduled pickup.

The front window of the bag will clearly display:

  • Order details and quantity
  • Your agreed-upon portioning diagram
  • Pickup time
  • A check attached for the total amount due

What Not to Include

No bags, napkins, cutlery, or condiments that weren't made in-house specifically for the entrée. We handle all of that in the basket — guests arrive with everything they need.

Box Pickup

We come to your service entrance to pick up the thermal bag with completed Entrée Boxes promptly at 11am or 5pm. Your kitchen's work is done — we take it from there.

A Day in the Life

8am

Receive advance notice of exact order quantity (lunch orders)

10am

We drop off sanitized boxes + check at your service entrance

11am

We pick up completed Entrée Boxes. Your work is done.

11:30am

Your food arrives at Art Hill. Picnickers meet your dish for the first time.

Dinner service follows the same pattern: 2pm notice → 4pm drop-off → 5pm pickup → 5:30pm at Pagoda Lake
