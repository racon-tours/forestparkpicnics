The Experience

The most romantic picnic in St. Louis

Pagoda Lake sits tucked inside Forest Park near The Muny — peaceful, lush, and just far enough from the crowd to feel like your own little world. As the evening light filters through the trees, it's hard to think of a better backdrop for a date night in St. Louis.

We handle everything. A fully stocked basket arrives ready — three courses from some of our favorite local restaurants in the CWE and Grove. Blanket, cutlery, corkscrew — all there. Two hours, just the two of you.

And with every booking, $5 goes to Forest Park Forever. Romance, but make it civic.

FAQ

Questions? We've got answers.

What happens if the weather is bad?

Free cancellation is available up to 24 hours before your picnic. If weather looks rough the day of, email us and we'll work something out.

What restaurants is the food from?

We rotate monthly with a curated selection of local CWE and Grove restaurants. Every month brings something new — check back or subscribe to our email list for menu updates.

Do I need to bring anything?

Just yourselves. We bring everything else. If you'd like to BYOB wine or a special beverage, there's a corkscrew in the basket waiting for you.

Are there dietary restriction options?

We curate each basket to include at least a little something for a variety of dietary walks of life — but we're unable to accommodate specific dietary requests. If you have a severe allergy or restriction, please reach out before booking.

Where exactly do we meet?

We'll send you exact meeting point details after you book. Generally, look for us at Pagoda Lake near The Muny at 5:30pm.
